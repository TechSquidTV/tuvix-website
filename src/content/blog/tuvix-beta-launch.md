The internet wasn't always about algorithmic feeds and engagement metrics. It used to be a collection of personal blogs, niche communities, and independent publishers. RSS gave us the power to follow what we wanted, not what an algorithm thought we should see.

**Tuvix is built on a simple belief: everyone deserves control over their information diet.** You shouldn't need to be a developer or tech expert to have a better internet experience. ## What's Included in Beta

### Core Features

- **Clean Reading Experience**: Distraction-free interface focused on contentâ€”no clutter, no noise
- **Chronological Feeds**: No algorithms deciding what you see. Just content as it's published, in order
- **OPML Import/Export**: Already using another RSS reader? Bring all your feeds over in seconds
- **Folder Organization**: Organize feeds however makes sense to youâ€”by topic, by priority, or however you think
- **Keyboard Shortcuts**: Navigate quickly without touching your mouse (for those who want it)

- **Hosted Option**: Free hosted version with minimal tracking (just Sentry for error monitoring to help us improve)
- **Self-Hosting**: Want complete control? Host it yourself for zero tracking
- **No Data Sales**: We'll never sell your data. Ever.
